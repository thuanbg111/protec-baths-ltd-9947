Governance and oversight

PROTEC BATHS LTD is managed by its directors, who are responsible for ensuring that the company complies with UK law and meets its contractual obligations to clients.

  • The company maintains statutory registers of directors and shareholdings.
  • Annual confirmation statements and other filings are submitted to Companies House when required.
  • Contracts with clients are agreed in writing and signed on behalf of the company.
  • Records of services delivered are retained to evidence the work completed.
  • Communication with partners and regulators is conducted using the official contact details.

Oversight in practice

An outline of the company’s typical governance cycle.

  1. 1. Planning and approval
    Directors consider proposed work, including scope, risks and resources.
  2. 2. Agreements signed
    Once approved, written agreements are signed and work can begin.
  3. 3. Monitoring delivery
    Progress is monitored and recorded; adjustments are agreed with the client if needed.
  4. 4. Reporting & record-keeping
    A summary of work is produced and stored alongside the supporting records.
